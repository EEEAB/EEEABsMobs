# Changelog v0.98

<u>AI translation may contain ambiguities, it is recommended to review in conjunction with the Chinese content.</u>

## New Additions or Changes

- Added new building blocks, materials, and weapons.
- Added natural generation of Ancient Boundary Stone between Y-level -48 and -64 in the Overworld.
- Added hostile mobs: Relic Ripper, Relic Annihilator, Realmwarden.
- Added pop-up notification feature, currently only used for in-combat prompts.
- Added custom boss health bar feature, allowing custom texture for a specific boss via resource packs.
- Moved unfinished content to the "Unfinished Content" item tab.
- Categorized hostile mob sound effects under the Hostile Creatures category.
- Cooldown and effectiveness of certain mob abilities are now affected by game difficulty.
- Redesigned some textures and adjusted their file paths.
- Updated certain mobs, including new abilities, balance adjustments, texture/particle changes, etc.
- Adjusted rarity for some items.
- Mod configuration updates:
  - Removed: unfinished item related config, Frenzy Potion block breaking config, and other old options.
  - Added client-side config: separately adjustable camera shake, boss music, falling block rendering, pop-up notifications, etc.
  - Boss combat mechanics: added effective attack distance, post-hit damage reduction duration, etc.
  - Directly set base damage for special entities or projectiles spawned by individual mobs.
- ...

### Frenzy Potion Adjustments

- Removed dash effect, 10% damage reduction per level, and maximum step height attribute.
- Reduced speed bonus per level.
- Added +1 armor value per level.
- While active, saturation depletes faster (equivalent to Hunger I).
- Increased duration for brewed potions.
- Effect cannot be removed by milk or commands.

### The Immortal Update

- Damage cap adjustment: 25 → 30
- Attack logic rework: now uses new animation release system.
- Kill healing adjustment: 5% of target's max health + 2.5% of own max health (old 1%), capped at 10% of own max health (old 5%).
- Dynamic damage increase adjustment: when number of targets > 1, each target increases damage by 10% (old: +0.4 base damage), up to 50% (old cap 2 damage).
- Taking explosive damage while casting Death Ray or Traction Punch can interrupt the ability; two interruptions cause stun and reset adaptation value.
- Reduced difficulty of dodging Immortal Shuriken.
- Damage from the sides reduced by 30% (old 50%).
- Parry counterattack mechanism adjustments:
  - Activation: Attempts to parry when idle, not on cooldown, damage from front and >1 point. Also, when below 50% health and playing an interruptible animation, will actively enter parry state.
  - Counter conditions (any of the following):
    - Single hit deals >7.5% of max health.
    - Takes more than 5 hits during parry.
    - Cumulative damage taken during parry >10% of max health.
    - Fully adapted to the damage.
  - Counterattack stuns nearby mobs and adds 50% adaptation value to the damage source.
- Damage adaptation mechanism:
  - Before fully adapting to the damage, the adaptation value gradually decays over time.
  - Removed "max adapted damage type count" limit, replaced by time-based clearance of expired adaptation records.
  - Adaptation value calculation is now based on multiple factors: damage percentage of max health, damage frequency, current health percentage, number of targets, etc., rather than fixed increments.
  - Added tag `#bypasses_damage_adapt`; damage with this tag (default includes void damage and command damage) ignores adaptation reduction.
  - Player attacks now differentiate between direct and indirect damage; special damage like Thorns and explosions now have separate adaptation keys.
- ...

### Boss General Damage Mechanism Adjustments

- Damage with tags `#bypasses_invulnerability` or `#bypasses_damage_cap` bypasses damage cap.
- Damage interval protection mechanism rework: repeated damage within a set time is reduced proportionally based on attack interval. Damage with tags `#bypasses_invulnerability` or `#bypasses_hurt_reduction` bypasses this reduction.

### 狂乱药水调整

- 移除了冲刺效果、每级 10% 的减伤效果、最大行走高度属性
- 降低每级提升的速度加成
- 新增每级增加 1 点护甲值
- 在效果持续期间，饱食度会掉得更快（效果等同于饥饿1级）
- 延长炼制出的药水时长
- 该效果无法被牛奶或者指令移除

### 不朽者更新

- 限伤调整：25  →  30
- 出招逻辑重做：应用了新的动画释放系统
- 击杀回血调整：目标最大生命值5%+自身最大生命值2.5%（旧1%），最高不超过自身最大生命值10%（旧5%）
- 动态增伤调整：目标数量＞1时，每个目标增加10%伤害（旧增加0.4点基础伤害），上限50%（旧上限2点伤害）
- 在释放【死亡射线】或者【牵引拳】时受到爆炸伤害可打断招式，累计打断2次可使其进入眩晕状态，并清空适应值
- 降低不朽手里剑的躲避难度
- 受到来自两侧的伤害减少 30%（旧50%）
- 格挡反击机制调整：
  - 触发：在无动作、未处于技能冷却、伤害来源来自正面且伤害值大于1点时尝试格挡。此外，在血量低于50%时若正在播放可打断的动画，也会主动进入格挡状态
  - 反击条件（只需满足下列任意一项）：
    - 单次造成伤害大于最大生命值 7.5%
    - 格挡期间受到超过5次攻击
    - 格挡期间累计伤害大于最大生命值 10%
    - 完全适应伤害
  - 反击会对近距离生物施加眩晕效果，并且对该伤害源增加50%的适应值
- 伤害适应机制：
  - 在未完全适应伤害前适应值会随时间逐渐衰减
  - 移除“最大适应伤害类型数量”限制，改为基于时间清除过期的适应记录
  - 适应值计算现在基于伤害占最大生命值比例、伤害频率、当前生命值比例、目标数量等多重影响，而非固定增量
  - 新增标签`#bypasses_damage_adapt`，加入此标签的伤害（默认包含虚空伤害和指令伤害）可无视适应减免
  - 玩家攻击会根据直接或间接伤害区分键值；荆棘、爆炸等特殊伤害现在有独立适应键值
- ...

### Boss通用受伤机制调整

- 当伤害加入标签 `#bypasses_invulnerability` 或 `#bypasses_damage_cap` 时，可无视限制伤害上限
- 伤害间隔保护机制重置：对设定时间内造成多次伤害时会根据攻击间隔对伤害进行一定比例的减少，当伤害加入标签 `#bypasses_invulnerability` 或 `#bypasses_hurt_reduction` 时，可无视伤害减免
